Logging surfaces in tt_bot
==========================

Formatter / handler
- Default formatter: "%(asctime)s %(levelname)s:%(name)s:%(message)s"
- All loggers below share the console handler installed in _configure_console_logging().

_TT (main TT bot)
- [CONFIG] L=MEGA E=MEGA-USD minSpread=0.4 spreadTP=0.2 repriceTick=0 maxPosValue=500 minHits=3 dedup=False warmUp=False maxTrades=None
- [DECISION MADE] abc123 TT_LE entry
- [BOTH VENUES FILLED]
- [FILLED LIG] abc123
- [FILLED EXT] abc123
- [INIT] L:0.0@0.0 | E:0.0@0.0 | Î”:0.0000%

DB (decision/trade/fill persistence)
- [INSERT DECISION] abc123
- [INSERT TRADE] abc123 L
- [INSERT FILL] abc123 L 0.25 128.39
- [ERROR INSERT DECISION]
- [ERROR INSERT TRADE]
- [ERROR INSERT FILL]

Venue helpers
- LIG / EXT log websocket/REST activity (not detailed here; see respective helper files). They remain wired to the console handler.

Other
- Hedge logger is attached to console but unused in TT-only mode.
- CSV-style Trades logger is disabled; no CSV rows are emitted.
